Dr. Vegapunk

Dr. Vegapunk, the brilliant and enigmatic founder of the Super Science Group and a former top scientist of the World Government, is a Mobel Peace Prize-winning mind whose creations defy imagination — from making flowers bloom from gunpowder, to decoding the secrets of pyrobloin and climate manipulation, crafting synthetic seastone applications, attempting to replicate the mythical Fish-Fish Fruit, Model: Azure Dragon, and engineering the fearsome Pacifista: mass-produced, laser-equipped cyborgs modeled after Bartholomew Kuma — precursors to the even more terrifying Seraphim, a new generation of bio-engineered superweapons.
